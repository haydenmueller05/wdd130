Our purpose

Our purpose is to make an app for everyone to easily access information and tools to improve your physical health according to their own goals. Often the simplest calculations are typically pay walled by companies to make money from their customers. We however, offer those tools and resources for free. We believe that people shouldn’t have to pay to better their health and that these resources should be easily accessible.

Tracker

Our Tracker is an online gym journal. It aims to help you to visualize your process and to push yourself to your physical limits for optimal muscle growth. It is a free and easily accessible tool that can help you achieve your personal goals for working out.

Try Tracker

Calculator

Our Calculator is an online caloric calculator. It calculates your daily calorie intake, based on the many factors given by the user. It gives you daily calorie suggestions based on the users goals. We aim to help give people the nutrition advice with no cost.
